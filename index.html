Field Research

Talgao Village, Uttar Pradesh, India

February 2026 40kW Microgrid TPRMG Socio-Economic Study

In February 2026, I conducted an on-site socio-economic impact assessment of a 40kW standalone solar microgrid in Talgao Village, Sitapur District, Uttar Pradesh — operated by Tata Power Renewable Microgrid Limited (TPRMG).

The visit provided ground-truth validation for my theoretical sizing models, gathering real load data, tariff structures, and direct beneficiary feedback. This field engagement is central to my research philosophy: engineering must serve real communities, not just equations.

The findings directly informed my PhD chapter on adaptive hybrid renewable energy microgrids, integrating socio-economic growth modelling with techno-economic optimization and AI-driven predictive maintenance.

Data Science & Energy Analytics Projects

RESEARCH · ONGOING
PV–BESS Capacity Estimation Model using TGY Data
Shizuoka University · HOMER Pro Validated
  • Regression models estimating required PV capacity as a function of normalized Typical Global Horizontal Irradiance Year (TGY) and rural load characteristics
  • Compared estimated vs HOMER Pro outputs across multiple cities & climatic zones; analysed absolute deviations |ΔPV| and correlation with GHI variability (SD, range)
  • Simplified sizing approach suitable for early-stage BESS planning in data-scarce regions
RESEARCH · PUBLISHED
Load Variability Analysis for Microgrid Component Sizing
Time-Series Analysis · MATLAB / Python
  • One-year minute-level load data aggregated to hourly values; designed variability indices TSV and D2DV
  • Quantified how variability patterns affect optimal PV/BESS capacity, diesel generator operation, and net present cost (NPC)
  • Established when simplified load profiles suffice vs. when high-resolution data is critical for reliable BESS design

Rural Microgrid Techno-Economic Optimization — Tanzania
